AFFOGATO

AFFOGATO (2)

Affogato means 'drowned' in Italian, which is because the ice cream is covered in an espresso shot. A true crowd pleaser that's easy to make!

Prep: 5 minutes.

  • Ingredients
  • Required
  • Steps
  • 2-3tsp NESCAFÉ AZERA Craft Coffee brewed with 200ml of freshly boiled water
  • 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream (can be substituted for dairy free alternative) (Contains milk)
  • 1 piece chocolate chopped or grated chocolate
  • Small knife
  • Ice cream scoop or spoon
  • 2 glasses, to serve
  • Allergens in this recipe: Milk
  1. In a chilled glass or dessert dish, place a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  2. Slowly pour the freshly brewed NESCAFÉ AZERA Craft Coffee over the ice cream.
  3. Garnish with chopped chocolate and serve immediately.

CAPPUCCINO

CAPPUCCINO (1)

This simple, delicious recipe will have a cappuccino in your mug without a coffee machine, just the robust flavour of NESCAFÉ.

Prep: 5 minutes.

  • Ingredients
  • Required
  • Steps
  • 1-2 tsps (2-4g) NESCAFÉ Original
  • 30ml (2 tbsps) freshly boiled water
  • 190ml semi-skimmed milk
  • Nutmeg, cinnamon, or cocoa powder (optional)
  • Glass container
  • Milk frother
  • Mug, to serve
  • Allergens in this recipe: Milk
  1. Mix the instant coffee and freshly boiled water. Stir it well and pour it into your mug of choice.
  2. Heat the milk in a glass container for 1 minute in a microwave and give it a stir once it is warmed up.
  3. Froth the hot milk for 2 minutes with a handheld frother. You are aiming for a foam layer that is 2 fingers thick, 35mm, to be precise.
  4. Pour the frothed milk into the coffee.
  5. Sprinkle with nutmeg, cinnamon, or cocoa, and serve immediately.

ICED CAFE MOCHA

ICED CAFE MOCHA (2)

Create a wonderfully tasty drink with our iced cafe mocha recipe. Perfect for your morning coffee break or as an afternoon treat.

Prep: 5 minutes.

  • Ingredients
  • Required
  • Steps
  • 6tsp NESCAFÉ GOLD BLEND brewed with 200ml of freshly boiled water
  • 200ml semi-skimmed milk or dairy-free alternative
  • 50g white chocolate chopped or white chocolate buttons (contains milk)
  • 2tbsp lightly whipped cream (optional)
  • Nutmeg
  • Milk frother
  • Heavy bottom pot
  • Whisk
  • Small grater
  • 2 mugs, to serve
  • Allergens in this recipe: Milk
  1. Add the milk and the white chocolate chips to a heavy bottom pot. Whisk together, on medium-low heat, until the white chocolate has melted.
  2. Add your freshly brewed NESCAFÉ GOLD BLEND to a cup or mug. Top up with the white chocolate milk and stir.
  3. If using top with lightly whipped cream and freshly grate nutmeg over the top.

MOCHA FRAPPE

MOCHA FRAPPE (2)

There's nothing better than a cooling mocha frappé on a hot day, so why not make your own? You can even add a dollop of cream for a perfectly sweet taste.

Prep: 5 minutes.

  • Ingredients
  • Required
  • Steps
  • 4-8tsp NESCAFÉ AZERA My Way Latte brewed with 400ml of freshly boiled water and left to cool
  • 100ml semi-skimmed milk or dairy-free alternative
  • 2tbsp sugar syrup (mix 2tbsp sugar with 1tbsp boiling water, bring to the boil and stir until dissolved)
  • 2 handfuls ice cubes
  • 2tbsp lightly whipped cream (optional)
  • Blender
  • 2 glasses, to serve
  • Allergens in this recipe: Milk
  1. Add all the ingredients apart from the cream to a blender.
  2. Blend until foamy and the ice is broken down completely.
  3. Pour into a glass and spoon over the lightly whipped cream if using.

MOCHA MOUSSE

MOCHA MOUSSE (1)

Stay cool! Our NESCAFÉ Mocha Mousse will refresh your body, mind, and soul.

Prep: 5 minutes.

  • Ingredients
  • Required
  • Steps
  • 1-2 tsps (2-4g) NESCAFÉ GOLD BLEND
  • 30ml (2 tbsps) freshly boiled water
  • 110ml cold semi-skimmed milk
  • 7 medium ice cubes
  • 2 tsps (10ml) brown sugar (optional)
  • Glass container
  • Allergens in this recipe: Milk
  1. Mix the instant coffee with the hot water. Add the sugar, if you are using it. Stir it well and set it aside.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Slowly pour in the coffee.
  4. Slowly stream in the milk.

SALTED CARAMEL LATTE

SALTED CARAMEL LATTE (1)

An indulgently sweet drink with rich aromas of caramel, and the bold, invigorating flavours of NESCAFÉ.

Prep: 5 minutes.

  • Ingredients
  • Required
  • Steps
  • 1-2 tsps (2-4g) NESCAFÉ instant coffee
  • 30ml (2 tbsps) freshly boiled water
  • 130ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 15ml sugar free caramel sauce
  • 1 tbsp whipped cream (optional)
  • Glass container
  • Milk frother
  • Mug, to serve
  • Allergens in this recipe: Milk
  1. Mix the instant coffee with the hot water. Stir it well and set it aside.
  2. Combine the milk and caramel sauce in a glass container. Heat it for 1 minute in a microwave, until bubbles just appear. Give it a stir to mix it properly.
  3. Add the coffee brew to the caramel milk. Froth it for 30 seconds with a handheld frother to get the perfect velvety texture.
  4. Pour the Salted Caramel Latte into your cup.
